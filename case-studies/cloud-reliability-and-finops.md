# Cloud Reliability, Automation, and FinOps

## Summary
Led or supported reliability-focused infrastructure work aimed at making enterprise platforms more stable, more supportable, and less wasteful to run.

## Context
- Environment type: enterprise healthcare technology
- Role focus: site reliability engineering, cloud optimization, automation, observability, incident response
- Public-safe framing: organizational specifics are reduced, but the technical themes are accurate

## Problem
Large enterprise platforms tend to accumulate cost, fragility, and operational noise over time. Unsupported recovery patterns, inconsistent automation, and weak observability increase both spend and incident risk.

## Constraints
- systems with real operational impact
- multiple teams and divisions involved in support and recovery
- need to improve reliability without creating more operational sprawl
- public writeup cannot expose internal platform architecture

## Actions
- drove infrastructure optimization work that reduced waste while preserving operational resilience
- helped plan the retirement of redundant or unsupported infrastructure patterns, including dozens of virtual machines
- built or improved event-driven recovery workflows using PowerShell-based automation
- standardized automation and remediation patterns through more repeatable tooling and deployment practices
- improved observability by refining metrics and operational signal quality
- supported incident review and corrective-action work grounded in telemetry and real operational evidence

## Tools / Methods
- Azure
- PowerShell
- Ansible Tower
- Dynatrace
- GitHub-based automation workflows
- incident analysis and operational standardization

## Result
- delivered more than $400K in annual cloud savings through optimization and rationalization work
- reduced redundant infrastructure and unsupported high-availability patterns
- improved consistency of recovery workflows across teams
- increased operational visibility and reduced diagnostic friction during incidents
- strengthened the long-term supportability of critical platforms
