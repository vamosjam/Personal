# Experience Highlights

This page is the quickest overview of the work themes behind the portfolio.
It is written for public sharing, so some details are generalized, but the underlying experience is real.

## Reliability and Automation

I have worked on production reliability, recovery design, and operational consistency in enterprise environments where downtime carries real consequences.
That work has included automation, observability improvement, incident follow-up, recovery planning, and platform stabilization.

Representative themes:
- event-driven recovery workflows
- operational automation with PowerShell and Ansible Tower
- observability improvement using custom metrics and better alerting signals
- incident analysis and durable corrective action
- standardization of operational processes across teams

## Cloud and Infrastructure Optimization

I have contributed to cloud and infrastructure optimization work intended to reduce waste while improving supportability and resiliency.

Representative themes:
- cloud cost reduction
- platform rationalization
- decommissioning of redundant or unsupported infrastructure patterns
- alignment of operational practice with supportable architecture

## Platform Ownership and Delivery

A large part of my background is owning platforms over time, not just touching them once.
That includes upgrades, vendor coordination, maintenance, implementation readiness, operational support, and modernization planning.

Representative themes:
- ownership of large application portfolios
- enterprise rollout support
- executive-facing technical analysis
- cross-functional execution with technical and operational stakeholders
- balancing long-term stability with near-term delivery needs

## Documentation and Risk Readiness

I have repeatedly turned fragile or inconsistent operational knowledge into documentation people could actually use.
I have also supported risk-aware review work where clarity, traceability, and evidence mattered.

Representative themes:
- runbook development
- workflow and data-flow documentation
- risk-oriented technical review
- support documentation and knowledge transfer
- making complex work easier to explain and sustain
